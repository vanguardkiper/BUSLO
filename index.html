About Us

Buslo studio started with our own intention of sharing our experience on how wooden and rattan products affect the atmosphere within our home and how it strengthen our relationship with mother nature. Through the creation of buslo studio, plants will no longer feel the loneliness of being abandoned as we give them their new home with our naturally hand-crafted products.
